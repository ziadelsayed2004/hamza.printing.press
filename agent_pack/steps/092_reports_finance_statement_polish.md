# 092 — Reports Finance Statement Polish

## Goal
Make reports and statements reflect corrected finance and shipping rules.

## Scope
- Reports must distinguish:
  - pending receivables.
  - collected.
  - supplied.
  - unsupplied.
  - shipped/partially shipped/not shipped.
- Outlet statements include invoice totals, collected, remaining, supplied status.
- Author reports show invoices containing their books.
- Outlet account reports show only that outlet's invoices and balances.

## Acceptance
- Reports match ledger totals.
- Arabic UI labels are clear.
- Exported reports match on-screen numbers.
